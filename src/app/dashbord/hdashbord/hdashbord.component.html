The Art and Discipline of Judo

Judo is more than just a sport—it's a discipline that builds strength, respect, and resilience. Introduced in Japan by Jigoro Kano in 1882, Judo focuses on leverage, balance, and technique rather than brute force. I had the honor of representing my college in Judo during my undergraduate studies, where I achieved a significant milestone by winning the **silver medal**. This experience not only shaped my physical abilities but also taught me the values of perseverance, humility, and mental discipline.
